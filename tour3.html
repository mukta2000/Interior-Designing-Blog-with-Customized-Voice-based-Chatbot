Jungle Vibes

When going green is a thing in the world right now why don’t our houses do the same .In the hills of Rio de Janeiro a family took this thought and well it happened

Bringing the outdoors till your doorstep this is exactly what happens here. One look and you will see exactly how lucky the kids of this house will be growing up in this lush paradise. Each room of the home takes full advantage of natural lighting, local elements, and of course the jade green vegetation that surrounds the house.

Even a glimpse of the home’s exterior brings a rush of excitement for the greenery that abounds. But the greenery just makes the house that much more intriguing — it is impossible to see those shades of green and not want to peek at what’s underneath.

An uncluttered design allows the eye to linger on each element from the rich wood flooring to the vintage-style furnishings and carefully arranged shelving. In lieu of large pieces of artwork, floating shelves turn the family’s books into their own work of art. A large dining table, open wooden steps overlook a very homey collection of wall hooks are just a few of the chic features of this house
