The Royal Living

This is not the usual types of homes we design and the budget was kind of hefty . But the outcome was surely impressive . Royalty has always been a liking for every one of us , more like our little secret all of us would love to be in a palace everyday ..but let’s face it none of us have the guts or the liberty to do so .

But the hamming’s in England didn’t quit agree with that and wanted their own royal , and so it started –“The Royal Living Project ” It was obvious we didn’t have the same space as you would have for a usual palace but we did make the most of the space provided for the couple .Even though the number of rooms were less but we made it all look royal Romantic wallpaper, gilded wainscoting in the loo, multilayered ceiling treatments with their own lighting schemes, and an inability to leave any void untouched

The Royal household has a personal touch which adds a little homeliness to the vast space .Royal household are known to be filled all time with enormously huge families and gathering all around the year but the Hamming’s knew that wouldn’t happen and hence decided to decorate the Royal in their own way . This house may not be the usual Royal Style or method but along with time I have grown to be pretty fond of it and the emotional value it stands upon
